Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AiCook collects only the information you voluntarily provide, such as ingredients you enter and photos you take for recipe recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide recipe recommendations based on the ingredients you have. Your data is processed locally on your device and sent to our servers only when necessary for generating recipes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized data for analytics purposes only.

4. Camera Permission

Our app uses the camera feature to allow you to take photos of ingredients. Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to take a photo.

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.
